● For Operators

Built by people who've been
on shift at 03:00.

Horizon is Maritime Coordination Intelligence built for the people who actually run the port and the waters around it. We use the words you use. We don't soften the work into marketing abstractions. And we don't replace your authority, we support it.

Who Horizon is for

One platform. Five rooms it shows up in.

A1 / HARBOUR MASTERS
Every conflict detected before it becomes an incident.

Harbour Masters are safety regulators first, coordinators second. Horizon delivers both. UKC violations, air-draught clearances, berth overlaps, tug double-bookings and pilot coverage gaps surfaced before the window closes, checked against every projected movement in the next 48 hours. Every movement backed by live tide, weather and draught data. Every decision logged and auditable.

Safety surfacingForward pictureAudit trail
A2 / VTS · PORT CONTROL
VTS tracks. Horizon coordinates above it.

Horizon reads from your VTS as one input into the coordination picture, it doesn't replace it. The forward picture sits on top, so the team can see what's coming, what's at risk, and which calls need to be made before the window closes.

A3 / PILOTS
Job sheet, briefing, conditions, in one app.

The companion app gives pilots their next assignments, the live conditions for each, and the upstream context that shaped the call. Less radio chatter. More clarity at the point of work.

A4 / TUG & LINES
Re-tasking before it becomes a phone call.

When a decision shifts the sequence, your dispatch system gets the update. Crew rosters, equipment allocations and time windows refresh together, not after three calls and a radio confirmation.

DispatchRe-taskingLive windows
A5 / TERMINALS
Berth windows that match the actual sequence.

Terminal operations see the same forward picture as the harbour. Berth allocations, gang plans and yard movements hold the same shape as port control's. One sequence. Not three reconciled by phone.
