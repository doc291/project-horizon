02 / Capabilities

Six modules. One operating picture.

M01 / FORWARD PICTURE
A shared 24-hour view, continually re-modelled.

Every vessel arrival, departure, berth swap and pilot exchange, projected forward and re-evaluated as each new signal lands. The forward picture is what every operator sees. There is no second version.

M02 / CONFLICT DETECTION
Safety-critical events, surfaced before commitment.

Under-keel clearance violations, air-draught against tide-adjusted bridge clearance, berth overlap and swing-room interference, the events that turn into incidents if they're seen late. The same engine catches the operational ones too: pilot coverage gaps, tug and line double-bookings, sequence conflicts. Each event evaluated at the point of variance, ranked by cascade impact, and held in front of the operator until it's resolved.

M03 / ETD RISK SCORING
Variance, scored live. Not a static ETA.

Every projected departure carries a live confidence band built from tide, weather, draft, service availability and upstream movement. As inputs shift, the score shifts. Operators see drift early, not at the window's edge.

M04 / DECISION RECOMMENDATIONS
The action to take, with the trade-off visible.

Each recommendation carries the alternatives evaluated, the cascade impact of each, and the window in which the decision must be made. The operator decides. Horizon supports.

M05 / COORDINATION TASKING
One source of tasking for pilots, tugs, lines.

When a decision is taken, the downstream tasking flows, to pilot rosters, tug operators, line handlers and port control. Service providers keep their tools. Coordination stops happening on the radio.

M06 / DECISION LEDGER
Every call, logged. Every action, traceable.

Immutable record of every decision, the data that informed it, the alternatives, and the operator who made the call. Audit trails built in, not bolted on. Regulator-ready by default.

03 / How it sits with your stack

A different category. Not a different version.

VTS was built to track vessels. Maritime Coordination Intelligence was built to coordinate ports. Different layers, different jobs. Horizon sits above your VTS, it doesn't sit beside it, and it doesn't replace it.

  • →
