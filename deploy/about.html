● About

Built by people who've
worked the bridge.

Horizon is the Maritime Coordination Intelligence division of AMS Group. We build software for the people who run ports and the waters around them, because we've sat in those rooms, on those shifts, with those radios.

The parent

AMS Group.

EST. 2004
MARITIME · LOGISTICS · SOFTWARE
HORIZON · DIVISION 01

AMS Group has spent two decades building software and operational tooling for the maritime industry. Our teams have worked alongside Harbour Masters, VTS operators, terminals, pilots and tug companies across multiple regions, a sector that moves 80% of global trade and over 11 billion tonnes of cargo a year.

Horizon is what happens when you take that operational fluency, add modern data infrastructure, and build a product around the conversation that already happens, instead of around the systems that don't talk to each other. To boards and investors, it's a Maritime Coordination Intelligence platform. To Harbour Masters, it's a shared forward picture for the port. To pilots, tugs and terminals, it's a coordination layer for maritime operations. Same product. Different rooms.

We're not a SaaS company that found maritime. We're a maritime company that ships software.

What we believe

Six things we'll argue for.

● 01
Operators decide.
Software supports the call. It never makes it. Every recommendation Horizon surfaces ends in a human approving or overriding it.
● 02
Tracking is not understanding.
A position on a screen is not insight. The category needs to grow up, from where vessels are, to what's about to go wrong.
● 03
Above, not instead of.
VTS was built to track. Horizon was built to coordinate. Existing systems work for a reason. We sit above them, connect them, and add the layer they were never built to provide.
● 04
Forward, not backward.
We lead with what's coming, not what happened. The next 24 hours is where decisions live. Reporting is a side-effect.
● 05
Audit by default.
Every decision logged. Every input recorded. The trail is the work, not a separate task to be reconstructed later.
● 06
Co-author, don't lecture.
We build with our partners, not for them. Founding port partners shape where the platform goes next.
